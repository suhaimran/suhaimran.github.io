Introduction

Who is Kehlani?

Kehlani Ashley Parrish was born on April 24th, 1995 (age 23). Kehlani is an American singer, songwriter, and dancer, signed to Atlantic Records. She is orginally from Oakland, California, where she first started her road to fame as a member of the teen group called Poplyfe. Her music includes the genres R&B, hip hop, and neo soul. Labels that represent her brand are the iconic "tsunami mob" and "Atlantic".

    Cloud 19
    		Cloud 19 is the debut mixtape by American singer and songwriter Kehlani, who released it as a free digital download on August 26, 2014, and is widely recognized as launching her music career. The mixtape ranked at twenty-eighth on Complex's list of the "50 Best Albums of 2014", and was also listed among Pitchfork's "Overlooked Mixtapes 2014".
    You Should Be Here You Should Be Here is the second and debut commerical mixtape by Kehlani. It was self-released through the iTunes Store on April 28, 2015. It peaked number 36 on the US Billboard 200. In July 2015, Kehlani embarked on a tour to promote the mixtape.
    SweetSexySavage is the debut studio album by Kehlani. The album was released on January 27, 2017 by Atlantic Records. Kehlani described the album as having three elements: Sweet, Sexy, and Savage, which are intended to be reflected in the album's lyrical content and production. The album received mainly positive reviews from critics, generation a Metacritic score of 76, which ranks as Kehlani's second-highest score after her mixtape You should be Here from 2015. It debuted at number three on the US Billboard 200. The record was promoted with three official singles, "Crzy", "Distraction", and "undercover"; the former two both peanked at number 85 on the Billboard Hot 100 and were certified gold by the RIAA. To further promote the album, Kehlani embarked on the SweetSexySavage Tour.
